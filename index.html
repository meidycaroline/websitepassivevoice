Pembelajaran: Passive Voice (Kalimat Pasif)

üìö Pengertian Passive Voice

Passive Voice adalah bentuk kalimat di mana subjeknya menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi.

Contoh:

  • Active: The chef cooks the meal.
  • Passive: The meal is cooked by the chef.

üõ† Struktur Passive Voice

Rumus: Subject + to be + V3 (past participle) + by + agent (optional)

Tense To Be Contoh Passive
Simple Present is, am, are The book is read by the student.
Simple Past was, were The window was broken by John.
Present Continuous is/am/are + being The cake is being made now.
Present Perfect has/have + been The letter has been sent.
Future will be The project will be finished soon.

Active Voice Passive Voice
She writes a letter. A letter is written by her.

He will open the door. The door will be opened by him.

‚ùó Kapan Menggunakan Passive Voice?

  • Jika pelaku tidak diketahui: The wallet was stolen.
  • Jika fokus pada aksi atau hasil: The homework is done.

üéØ Latihan Soal

Ubah kalimat berikut ke bentuk Passive Voice:

  1. The teacher explains the lesson.
  2. They build a new bridge.
  3. She has finished the report.
  4. People speak English worldwide.
  5. The students are watching a movie.

Jawaban:

  1. The lesson is explained by the teacher.
  2. A new bridge is built by them.
  3. The report has been finished by her.
  4. English is spoken worldwide.
  5. A movie is being watched by the students.
